MY NAME IS KYLE PETERS AND I AM A PHOTOGRAPHER AND VIDEOGRAPHER BASED OUT OF TORONTO CANADA. I HAVE A PASSION FOR URBAN ENVIRONMENTS THAT ALLOW ME TO SHOW BEAUTY IN THE MAN MADE WORLD.

I HAVE BEEN A PHOTOGRAPHER FOR 10 YEARS WORKING IN WEDDINGS VENUES, SPORTS EVENTS, AND FOR APPERAL COMPANIES BUT AFTER STUDYING VIDEOGRAPHY AND SHOTTING ION DOWNTOWN TORONTO, I FELL IN LOVE WITH THE BEAUTY OF THE CITY.

I NOW WORK AS A FREELANCER WORKING WITH MULTIPUL CLOTHING BRANDS THROUGHOUT NORTH AMERICA AND ENJOY EVERY MINUTE OF IT.
